Stone town

Stone town is a very colorful and fascinating port city, where African, Arab and Indian cultures are mixed. Words cannot convey the atmosfere of this place. I fell in love forever with its many old wonderful hotels and plenty of massive beautiful doors. Walking around the city used to put me in a fairytale...

You can find lots of interesting buildings, some of the are covered with black spots - it's mold. Due to hight humidity on the island there are a lot of mold as well.
