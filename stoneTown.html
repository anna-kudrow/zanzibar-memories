Stone town

Stone town is the old city of Zanzibar - a very colorful and fascinating port city, where African, Arab and Indian cultures are mixed. Words cannot convey the atmosfere of this place. I fell in love forever with its many old wonderful hotels and plenty of massive beautiful doors. Walking around the city used to put me in a fairytale...
